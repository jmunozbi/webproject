About me

Mathematician/Physicist specialising in computational modelling with a focus on biophysics. Over one year of experience developing Python-based models to gain insights into red blood cell shapes in flow. Additionally, I spent ten months abroad in KU Leuven, Belgium, where I was immersed in a PhD program centred around tissue engineering. In my research, I combined experimental measurements (AFM, TFM, NI...) with computer simulations (Python+Mpacts).

Passionate about the world of technology and innovation, I am always eager to expand my skill set and learn new programming languages to enhance my career.

You can have a look at my projects and contact me if you are interested in my profile.
